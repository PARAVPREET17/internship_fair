Guidelines

○ Make sure you have a proper internet connection throughout the event.
○ The interaction with a given company representative will proceed on a first-come-first-served basis.
○ Prepare your resume(a sample will be available on the Internship Fair's website) and keep a pdf of the same along with yourself.
○ Be dressed in formal/semi-formal attire and keep your camera on while interacting with a company representative.
○ The fair will commence at 11:00 am on April 25.
○ Registration for the Internship Fair will remain open on the day of the Fair as well.
○ Try to join as early as possible.
○ Strictly follow the job description and eligibility criteria while applying for a given profile.
○ EDC, TIET provides you with platform to approach the companies easily not a guaranteed internship.
○ An individual can apply for maximum of 8 internships only.
○ EDC does not stand liable for any legal dispute, whatsoever.
